Who Are We?



Legend Logistics is Africa's leading indigenous logistics company, powered by technology and renowned for its e-commerce and social commerce-tailored logistics.

Founded in 2020 with the conviction that e-commerce and social commerce are only possible through efficient logistics, we set out with a clear goal: to eliminate the conventional hassles of last-mile deliveries and make logistics seamless for individuals and businesses, anywhere and anytime.

Since our first shipment in 2020, Legend Logistics has grown exponetially, connecting African vendors with global buyers and sellers through our simplified overseas shipping services across several continents.

At Legend Logistics, we pour our heart and expertise into every detail, from packaging and handling to the final delivery and then to the end users. Our agility and commitment to service excellence, and data-centric approach enable us to offer lasting solutions to critical logistics challenges. We are also committed to ethical business practices, upholding our corporate, environmental and social responsibilities
